About DeTicktor

DeTicktor is an innovative early detection system specifically designed to identify ticks on pets before they become a serious health threat. Our technology serves veterinarians, animal shelters, and pet owners who want the best protection for their beloved companions.

Winning the Animal Health Hackathon at Cornell in February 2025, our solution represents the cutting edge of pet health technology, combining advanced detection methods with user-friendly design.
